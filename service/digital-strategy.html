Real Time Bidding

Real-time-bidding and real time media buying is a form of online advertisement that allows you to show your ads at the exact moment a person would most likely find that ad useful.

This strategy can be used for small businesses and medium to large businesses including corporations and nationwide service providers. Real Time Auction bidding feature of the platform allows marketers to target Ads at the most suitable time to target relevant audience.

Search Engine Optimization

Search Engine Optimization is a science that analyzes search engine algorithms and human visitor behavior to get your website top rankings on major search engines.

SEO is also the art of using Meta tags, organic positioning of keywords in the body text, analyzing keyword density using popular SEO tools and techniques.

Our experts will devise the most appropriate Search Engine Optimization strategy. Their approach is also based on research, target audience, website content, competitor analysis and other relevant data.

Mobile

Smartphones are the most common starting place for online activities. It’s not just about the frequency of smart phone usage but also about multi-screen usage.

Mobile is today the new “first screen”. As people move between screens, we deliver cohesive advertising experiences that move with people. We offer In app advertising to reach our audience with various targeting options. We target the right audience based on demographic data, location and mobile device where we deliver the right information to the right targeted audience at the right time.

Display Advertising

Display Advertising appears in the form of colorful banners and product ads that pop up in the middle of an article on your favorite blog or news site.

Images can be a powerful tool for advertisers. Combined with a call to action, a direct path to conversion, and specific targeting, Display Advertising can quickly become a highly profitable advertising initiative.
